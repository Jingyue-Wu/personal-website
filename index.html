My Work

Study Spot

Created at Deltahacks IX in 24 hours. Winner of "Most Creative Use of Verbwire API."

Study Spot a web application with an innovative solution to unproductivity through the implementation of gamification and web3. It features an automated study timer that uses geolocation to track the time students spend studying in libraries and awards points. A built-in leaderboard that tracks accumulated points enables users to compete with others around the world. Using the points, users can redeem NFT achievements and awards on the Ethereum blockchain.

Custom Vertical Axis Wind Turbine

First place winner of the McMaster Engineering 1P13 Renewable Technology Challenge

A unique vertical axis wind turbine based on the Darrius Model which has the ability to intake wind from all directions and maximizes efficiency in wind farms. This design minimized materials, making it much more cost effective and versatile compared to traditional turbines. Autodesk Inventor was used to design and optimize the model using deflection simulations.
